Why we exist

Science access, driven by curiosity instead of circumstance.

Every scientist remembers the moment they first felt wonder. Discovery Day began in 2025 after science budget cuts at Peterson Middle School, with a simple goal: bring that "wow" moment to students in under-resourced classrooms, where potential too often goes untapped.

🧪Real experiments

Students grow crystals, extract DNA from a banana, and watch carbon dioxide trap heat. Every lab is hands-on, and they learn the real science behind what they see.

🌎Built for every learner

Every class has equal parts images, visual instructions, explanations, and lessons, so everyone can follow along no matter how they learn best.

🤝Made with the teacher

Every workshop is tailored to your classroom and approved by you in advance, from the activity to the supplies.

For teachers

Bringing Discovery Day to your class is easy.

1Browse

Look through the workshop catalog and pick a lab that fits your class, or ask for a custom one.

2Plan together

We build the presentation, worksheet, and experiment around your students and supplies, and you approve it all in advance.

3We teach it

Our team brings the materials and runs the workshop. Timing is flexible, and it is always free.
